Strawberry Chicken Salad

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 6 cups mixed greens
  • 1 cup fresh strawberries, sliced
  • 1/2 cup cucumber, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup pecans or almonds
  • 1 ripe avocado, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil (for cooking the chicken)
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

For the dressing:

  • 3 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon honey (or maple syrup)
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Description

This vibrant Strawberry Chicken Salad is a fresh and flavorful mix of tender grilled chicken, juicy strawberries, and crisp mixed greens. Itâ€™s balanced with a light and tangy vinaigrette, complemented by crunchy nuts and creamy avocado. The salad offers a refreshing, sweet-savory combination perfect for a light lunch or a healthy dinner option.

Steps

  1. Season and cook the chicken until fully cooked, then slice it.
  2. Arrange the mixed greens, strawberries, cucumbers, red onions, pecans, and avocado in a bowl.
  3. Whisk together balsamic vinegar, honey, mustard, olive oil, salt, and pepper for the dressing.
  4. Place the sliced chicken on top of the salad and drizzle with the dressing.
  5. Toss gently and serve.
