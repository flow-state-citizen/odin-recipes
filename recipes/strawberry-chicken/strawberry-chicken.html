Strawberry Chicken Salad

    Ingredients for the Salad
  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 6 cups mixed greens
  • 1 cup fresh strawberries, sliced
  • 1/2 cup cucumber, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup pecans or almonds
  • 1 ripe avocado, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil (for cooking the chicken)
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

    • Ingredients for the Dressing
  • 3 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon honey (or maple syrup)
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Description

This vibrant Strawberry Chicken Salad is a fresh and flavorful mix of tender grilled chicken, juicy strawberries, and crisp mixed greens. It’s balanced with a light and tangy vinaigrette, complemented by crunchy nuts and creamy avocado. The salad offers a refreshing, sweet-savory combination perfect for a light lunch or a healthy dinner option.

Steps

  1. Prepare the Chicken
    • Season: Start by seasoning both sides of the chicken breasts with salt and pepper to taste. You can also add your preferred seasonings like garlic powder, paprika, or Italian seasoning for extra flavor.
    • Cook: Heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Once the oil is hot, add the seasoned chicken breasts to the skillet.
    • Sear: Cook for 6-8 minutes on each side, or until the chicken reaches an internal temperature of 165°F (75°C) and is golden brown on the outside.
    • Rest: Once fully cooked, transfer the chicken to a cutting board. Let it rest for about 5 minutes before slicing it into thin strips.

  2. Prepare the Salad Base
    • Wash: Thoroughly wash the mixed greens, strawberries, cucumber, and avocado.
    • Slice:
      • Strawberries: Slice 1 cup of fresh strawberries into halves or quarters, depending on their size.
      • Cucumber: Thinly slice 1/2 cup of cucumber into rounds.
      • Red Onion: Thinly slice 1/4 cup of red onion for a mild, crisp bite.
      • Avocado: Slice the ripe avocado just before serving to avoid browning.

  3. Make the Dressing
    • Whisk together the balsamic vinegar, honey, olive oil, Dijon mustard, and salt and pepper for the dressing.

  4. Assemble the Salad
    • Arrange the salad: In a large bowl or individual plates, lay down a bed of 6 cups of mixed greens. Evenly distribute the sliced strawberries, cucumbers, red onion, toasted nuts, and avocado slices over the greens.
    • Add the chicken: Place the sliced chicken breasts on top of the salad.
    • Dress the salad: Drizzle the dressing over the top. Use as much or little as you like.

  5. Toss and Serve
    • Toss the salad: Gently toss the salad to ensure the dressing coats all the ingredients evenly.
    • Serve: Plate the salad and enjoy!
