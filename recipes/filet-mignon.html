Recipe for Filet Mignon

Pan-Seared Filet Mignon

This recipe is for a classic filet mignon, known for its tenderness and rich flavor. The steak is lightly seasoned with salt and pepper, then seared to perfection in a hot cast-iron skillet, creating a beautiful crust. Afterward, it's finished in the oven to achieve the perfect level of doneness. A simple pan sauce made with butter, garlic, and fresh thyme enhances the steak’s natural flavor, making this dish a restaurant-quality meal that’s surprisingly easy to prepare at home. Whether served with roasted vegetables, mashed potatoes, or a fresh salad, this filet mignon will impress every time.

You can adjust the seasonings and cooking time to suit your preference, making it a versatile and elegant dish for any occasion.
